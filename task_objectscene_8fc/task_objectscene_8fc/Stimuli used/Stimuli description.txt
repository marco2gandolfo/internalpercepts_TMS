First character: Stimulus category - 	A: Airplane
					B: Bird
					C: Car
					F: Fish
					M: Mammal
					P: Person
					S: Ship
					T: Train

Second character: Stimulus type - 	A: degraded object in scene
					B: degraded object alone
					C: intact object alone
					D: scene alone

Third character: Old or new stimulus - 	N: New
					O: Old

Fourth and fifth character: number

BBN15: degraded bird alone from the new stimulus set, number 15
